## Problems to Avoid

- **Widows** – single word or very short last line at the end of a paragraph
- **Orphans** – single line isolated at the top of a new column or page
- **Rivers** – vertical channels of white space running through justified text
- **Poor rag** – distracting shapes in the right margin (steps, wedges, pyramids)
- **Uneven word spacing** – inconsistent gaps between words across lines
